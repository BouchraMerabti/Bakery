How to Order from Bouchras drömmar

1. Explore Our Delicious Menu

Begin your sweet journey by browsing our full selection of artisanal cookies and exquisite French pastries on our Our Artisanal Creations section. Discover detailed descriptions, tempting photos, and pricing for each delightful item.

2. Place Your Order

Ready to indulge? You can place your order easily by email or phone:

  • By Email: Send us an email at bouchrasdrommar@gmail.com. Please include:
    • Your desired items and quantities.
    • Your preferred pickup or delivery date.
    • Any special requests or dietary considerations.
  • By Phone: Give us a call at 0739762667. We're happy to assist you with your order and answer any questions.

3. Lead Time Required

To ensure the freshest and highest quality, we kindly request a minimum of 72 hours notice for all orders. For larger orders or custom requests, please contact us as early as possible to discuss arrangements.

4. Payment Methods

We accept the following secure payment methods:

  • Swish: (For customers in Sweden)
  • Bank Transfer: Details will be provided upon order confirmation.

5. Pickup & Delivery Options

Choose the option that works best for you:

  • Local Pickup (Malmö): Collect your freshly baked treats directly from our home kitchen in Malmö. We will confirm the exact address and available pickup times when you place your order.
  • Nationwide Dry Cookie Delivery (Sweden): We are delighted to offer delivery of our dry cookies to all of Sweden! A delivery fee will apply based on your location. Please inquire about delivery costs when placing your order.
  • Please note: Fresh pastries and delicate items are currently only available for local pickup in Malmö to ensure their quality upon arrival.

Bouchra's Dream: Baked with Love, A Taste of Home

From a young age, the kitchen was my sanctuary, a place where flour, butter, and sugar transformed into pure magic. What started as a childhood fascination quickly blossomed into a profound passion for baking, especially for the intricate artistry of French patisserie. "Bouchras drömmar," meaning Bouchra's Dreams, is truly a realization of that lifelong dream.

My journey into the world of French baking involved countless hours of learning, experimenting, and perfecting techniques passed down through generations. I've dedicated myself to understanding the delicate balance that creates that perfect flaky croissant or that melt-in-your-mouth macaron. Each recipe is approached with reverence for tradition, yet infused with my own personal touch and creativity.

What truly sets Bouchras drömmar apart is our unwavering commitment to quality. We meticulously select only the finest ingredients – from rich, pure butter to premium chocolates and fresh, local produce whenever possible. Every cookie, every pastry, is baked in small batches with genuine care and attention to detail, ensuring that when you taste a "Bouchras drömmar" creation, you're not just enjoying a treat, but experiencing a piece of heartfelt joy. Thank you for being a part of my dream!
